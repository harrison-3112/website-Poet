yellow hoa mai (Vietnamese apricot blossoms), white wild orchids, and soft pink milk flowers (hoa sữa).
Style: ink wash watercolor meets fine art photography. Very soft, dreamy, ethereal atmosphere.

Color palette:
- Dominant: warm ivory / aged paper (#f6f1e8, #fffdfc)
- Accent: golden amber (#ae8a5a), sage green (#64725f)
- Subtle shadow tones: dusty brown (#6b5b4d), pale mist (#d8d0c4)

The composition must work as a web page background:
- Very low contrast (no harsh darks or whites)
- Soft bokeh, out-of-focus flower petals
- Subtle paper grain / rice paper texture
- No text, no human figures
- Landscape orientation 16:9
- Painterly and poetic mood suitable for classical Vietnamese poetry

3. Trong globals.css, thêm vào body:

     background-image: url('/images/page-background.jpg');
     background-size: cover;
     background-attachment: fixed;
     background-position: center;
   }

   Hoặc thêm overlay để chữ luôn đọc được:

   body::before {
     content: "";
     position: fixed;
     inset: 0;
     background: rgba(246, 241, 232, 0.82);  /* --color-paper với độ mờ */
     pointer-events: none;
     z-index: 0;
   }
